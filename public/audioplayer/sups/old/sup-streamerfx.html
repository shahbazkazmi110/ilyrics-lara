FAQ

Most frequent questions and answers
What is Royalty Free?

In the entertainment industry, “royalties” are funds paid to an artist when their songs are used in Radio, TV & Movies. When music, video or images are listed as Royalty Free, you can use them without having to pay royalties to the artist. In other words, you’re free and clear as long as you stay within the licensing terms.

Why are these Songs more expensive?

As stated above, no royalties are being paid to the artist. In exchange for unlimited usage, you pay a little more when purchasing.

Where can I use this music?

As a Streamer or Content Creator you’re free to use these songs for:

Live Streams,
Social Media Videos (YouTube, Facebook, IG, TikTok, etc),
Commercials & PSAs,
and other media

Can I monetize my content?

Yes

Do I own this music?

Please note that StreamerFX / EnaTainment LLC holds exclusive rights to all original music listed on this site. You are simply being granted permission to use this music in your content. Please understand other creators have access to this music, but only we, (StreamerFX) have the right to prevent the use of it.

Should I acknowledge StreamerFX?

If you’re using a full song or loop, we ask that you list StreamerFX in the content description and credits. Example below.

Outro:
Song Title by StreamerFX
https://streamerfx.com

Can I purchase a song outright?

Yes you can. We offer membership as well as individual purchases. Whichever method you choose, you’ll always have access to your downloads by logging in to your account.
