Loopsbox is proud to present one of our best and most complete house music oriented packs. House Sessions consists of (5) 100% royalty-free unique construction kits. This means you can use everything in your new track and release it on the house music labels! Each kit is key labeled and comes with all of the individual one-shot hits of each track so that you can mix and rearrange to your liking. Construction kits not only contain incredible audio samples, they serve as phenomenal educational tools so that artists may see how other producers form and arrange their melodies and drum kits.
