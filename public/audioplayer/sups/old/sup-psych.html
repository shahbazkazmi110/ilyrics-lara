    Discovery Pro Psytrance Soundset Vol.2

    Doctor Doubledrop returns with other 128 high quality Psytrance presets for discoDSP Discovery Pro VST.
    The second volume of his amazing collection is now ready to be used in your projects! From old school sounds to modern synths this bank includes all the presets that you’ll need covering; leads, zaps, lasers, sirens, basses, groove, soundscapes, sequences, gates & FX! As a special bonus the download also includes 15 bonus MIDI files to launch your productions to instant Psychedelic heaven!

    Royalty-Free: All of the content in this download is 100% royalty-free. Once purchased, you can use these
    sounds in your own commercial music releases with no restrictions.

    Bank description:
    •51 Leads
    •06 Bass
    •09 Arp
    •07 Gat
    •10 Pad
    •06 Downlifter
    •06 Uplifter
    •06 Up&Down
    •27 FX
    •15 Midi

    Note:
    Bass used in demo: “BASS D&D4”
    Kick & grooves used in demo are not included in the bank.
    These presets require V5.6 or higher of discoDSP Discovery Pro Synth.
