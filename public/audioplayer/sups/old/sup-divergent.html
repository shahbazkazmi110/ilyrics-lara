Wavetable Synthesizer from Outter Space

WAVEFORMS

Combining 128 unique waveforms, from some of the rarest and most sought after analog and digital sources, Invasors creates a sound that is both unique and instantly recognizable. Seamlessly blending between warm and mellow pads to wild and intense leads and everything in between. With a dual voice engine, each with its own waveform, and the ability to quickly browse and randomize waveforms inspiration is endless!

(Un)limited Control

Wether youre a deep tweaker or a hardware minimalist INVASORS can be setup to work exactly the way you want. Every parameter is controllable from any MIDI controller with 8 knobs or faders. All pages, parameters and controls can be navigated instantly. This means a faster workflow and a truly hardware experience in a software instruments. For users ofMaschineandKomplete Kontrol, INVASORS is designed from the ground up as anNKSinstrument. This allows the instant integration and the highest level of control over every parameter.

TRUE ANALOG

Every aspect of INVASORS brings the true experience of an analog synthesizer to your favourite audio software. From its realistic 3D rendered interface to its detailed collection of waveforms which have been expertly sampled and looped. Process the sound with a virtual rack of effects. Combine four unique filters and modulate them all with unique combinations of LFOs and Envelopes. If thats not enough take it even further and sequence everything or browse through the included bank of 256 expertly designed presets. Wether working in the studio or the stage INVASORS brings your production to a whole new world that blurs the line beyond software and hardware synthesis.

CORE Features
  • 128 Waveforms per Oscillator – Easily create new and unique sounds by blending 128 unique waveforms from some of the rarest and most sought after analog and digital sources.
  • MIDI CONTROLLABLE – Every parameter is instantly controllable from any MIDI controller with 8 knobs or faders. Go deep with instant mapping for Native Instruments Komplete Kontrol or Maschine hardware.
  • MIDI RECORD – Record new patterns from your MIDI controller and export them using the built-in Drag & Drop as sequences compatible with any external software or hardware synthesizer.
  • 256 Patches Bank – Browse the collection of 256 expertly designed presets or go deep by exploring the wealth of parameters able to fulfil the needs of even the most experienced sound designer. Snapshots are Rename-able.
  • Realistic Interface – A 3D rendered interface provides the true experience of working on a hardware synth inside your favourite audio software.
  • Process the sound with a virtual rack of effects, four unique filters and modulate them all with unique combinations of LFOs, Envelopes and powerful Step Sequencer.
  • SEQ Randomizer – Use INVASORS intelligent Sequencer randomizer to create instant inspiration by instantly create new and unique patterns.

Re-

INVASORSfeatures a one of a kind and infinitely powerful step sequencer. Take full control and create pitched sequences, monophonic glides, filtered sweeps, LFO modulations and per step effect changes. Or put INVASORS in charge with the ability to intelligently randomize every parameter and blend instantly between classic monophonic sequences and futuristic sounds unlike anything youve ever heard. With INVASORS MIDI export function you can drag and drop patterns from its internal sequencer to other software and hardware synths. Create new and interesting ideas with your favourite instruments for infinite inspiration.
