The Miraculous Power of Jesus

Video
Tweet

This series’ foothold is based on the belief that Jesus’ miracles were not for the purpose of converting, but to give proof that he was the Son of God come with power. This had to be this way since the miraculous signs were the only way to confirm to the Jews that the prophet was sent from God (John 6:30, 1 Corinthians 1:22). Through these lessons, the student will learn that the miraculous signs first proved that Jesus Christ came from God, and then later, what was taught by the Apostles was the Word of God (Mark 16:20, Hebrews 2:3,4).

1. Doble Milagro: Perdón y Sanación Double Miracle: Forgiveness and Healing
2. Jesús Somete a la Naturaleza Jesus, Master of Creation
3. Jesús Derrota a Satanás Jesus Overcomes Satan
4. Jesús derrota la enfermedad y la muerte Jesus Overcomes Infirmity And Death
5. Jesús Derribó el Imperio de la Muerte Jesus Defeats Death s Empire
